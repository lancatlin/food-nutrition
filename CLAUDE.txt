# Food Nutrition App

## Background

According to multiple research papers (from 2009 to 2022), international students around the world have been facing a common health concern - **food malnourishment**.

While many factors such as economic crisis and stress due to work are identified as causes, a lack of information about nutritional concepts has been identified as a common cause. Poor diet quality leads to malnutrition or overnutrition, increasing the risk of preventable diseases such as diabetes and obesity.

## Solution

Develop an application that follows the workflow below:

1. Use OCR to extract food items from the user’s grocery list
2. The food items are stored in a database. The user can (optionally) manually add the expiry date for food items that will expire soon.
3. Our AI then creates healthy, diabetic-friendly recipes while prioritizing items that will be expiring soon.
4. It also return the nutritional value of the recipe per 100g so the user can keep track of his calories, protein, etc…
